How it Works



Functionality


Welcome to Feast.fm,

A search will return a list of artists, labels, albums, and tracks matching your search.

Albums and tracks are playable on the page. If you click on an artist or a label you will be directed to a page showing their entire discography. When you play a track the player will attempt to find the track on hosts such as YouTube and SoundCloud. As you find albums and tracks you like you can to add them to your collection or click to add them to a playlist.

- Finds the album tracklist and shows it underneath the album.

- Gets the album tracklist and plays the first track

- Plays an individual track.

- Saves a track or album to your collection. You can see saved tracks and albums by clicking on "My Music" and then "Songs/Albums" on the top of the page.

- Shows a list of playlists to which you can add a track or album, also gives you the option of creating a new playlist. Simply click the button next to the playlist you want the song or album to go into. You can see your playlists by clicking "My Music" > "Playlists" at the top of the page.

Follow artists and labels by clicking on the button next to their names. Artists and labels that you are already following will have a button next to their names. You can see a list of artists and labels that you follow in "My Music" > "Artist/Labels/Sites".

Stream - shows new releases by artists and labels that you follow. You can get to your stream by following the link at the top of the page.
